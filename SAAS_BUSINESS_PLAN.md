# PhotoPrint Studio SaaS - Business Plan & Go-to-Market Strategy

## Executive Summary

Transform your single-business photo printing app into a **white-label SaaS platform** that other photo printing businesses can use, branded with their own logos and connected to their own payment processors.

**Market Opportunity:** $2B+ global photo printing market with high demand for digital solutions.

---

## Product Vision

### **What You're Building**

A **Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform** that allows photographers, studios, and print businesses to:
- ✅ Create their own branded photo printing storefront
- ✅ Customize with their logo, colors, and text
- ✅ Connect their own payment processor (Stripe, Shopify, Square)
- ✅ Manage orders through their own admin dashboard
- ✅ Track orders with professional numbering system
- ✅ Ensure photo quality before printing

### **Your Revenue**

You charge tenants (businesses) to use your platform through:
1. **Subscription fees** ($29-299/month)
2. **Commission per transaction** (2-10%)
3. **Setup fees** for custom configurations
4. **Premium features** (custom domains, advanced analytics)

## Business Model

### **Pricing Strategy Option 1: Subscription Tiers**

**Starter - $29/month**
- 10 orders/month
- Basic customization (logo, colors)
- Stripe or Shopify
- Community support

**Professional - $99/month**
- Unlimited orders
- Advanced customization
- Multiple payment processors
- Priority email support
- Audit reports
- Advanced analytics

**Enterprise - $299/month**
- Everything in Professional
- Custom domain (myprints.com)
- API access
- Dedicated account manager
- Phone support
- Custom integrations

### **Pricing Strategy Option 2: Commission-Based**

**Free Account**
- Setup for free
- You take 8% commission per transaction
- Stripe payment only
- Basic customization

**Pro Account - $29/month**
- Flat fee $29/month
- You take 2% commission
- Multiple payment processors
- Advanced features

### **Pricing Strategy Option 3: Hybrid**

**Base Cost:** $15/month
**Commission:** 3% per transaction
**Premium Features:** Extra $20-50/month

## Funding Options

### **Self-Funded (Bootstrap)**
- Use early customer revenue
- Reinvest profits
- Takes longer but maintains control
- Recommended for SaaS

### **Friends & Family Round**
- $50K-100K from investors you know
- Retain significant equity
- Flexible terms
- Good for reaching MVP+

### **Angel Investors**
- $100K-250K
- Give up 10-20% equity
- Faster growth
- Lose some control

### **Venture Capital**
- $500K+
- Expect 20-40% dilution
- High growth expectations
- Complex fundraising process

**Recommendation:** Bootstrap or friends & family initially.
